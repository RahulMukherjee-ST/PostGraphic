How cops kept their badges

Of 629 cases tracked by the Star Tribune, about half started with felonies or gross misdemeanors – offenses that can end an officer’s career. As they moved through the court system, more than one-third of these were reduced to misdemeanor convictions that triggered no board review or discipline.
